Cookie Policy

Learn how Find Your Morocco uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your website experience.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

How We Use Cookies

Find Your Morocco uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly, including navigation and access to secure areas.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website by collecting anonymous information about page visits and traffic sources.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences (such as language settings) to provide enhanced features.
  • Targeting Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party services to place cookies on your device for:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics to understand website traffic and user behavior
  • Social Media: Facebook, Instagram, and other platforms for social sharing and tracking
  • WhatsApp: For direct communication and booking functionality

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways:

  • Adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being set
  • Use browser tools to delete existing cookies from your device
  • Note that blocking certain cookies may impact website functionality

Cookie Duration

Session cookies expire when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. We typically use persistent cookies with durations ranging from 30 days to 2 years.

Your Consent

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page.
