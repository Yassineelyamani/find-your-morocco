Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how Find Your Morocco collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Introduction

Find Your Morocco ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website findyourmorocco.com or use our luxury Morocco tour services.

Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in various ways, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, travel dates, and other details you provide when contacting us via WhatsApp or our contact form.
  • Travel Preferences: Destinations of interest, group size, accommodation preferences, and special requirements.
  • Communication Data: WhatsApp messages, emails, and other communications you send to us.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and customize your luxury Morocco tour experience
  • Communicate with you about your travel plans via WhatsApp or email
  • Process bookings and reservations
  • Improve our services and website
  • Send you relevant travel information and updates (with your consent)

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners such as hotels, riads, desert camps, and transport providers who need your information to fulfill your travel arrangements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us via WhatsApp at +212 673 628 871 or through our contact form.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
