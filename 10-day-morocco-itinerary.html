10-Day Morocco Itinerary, The Grand Circuit

This sample 10-day Morocco itinerary covers imperial cities, the High Atlas, Erg Chebbi dunes and the Blue Pearl — all by private vehicle with luxury riads and a premium desert camp. Adjust any day to match your flight times and interests.

Why Ten Days in Morocco

A 10-day Morocco itinerary is the sweet spot for international travelers: enough time for culture, desert and scenery without rushing. Every day below can be extended, shortened or reordered.

  • Covers Morocco's most requested highlights
  • Private driver & guide throughout
  • Luxury riads + premium Sahara camp
  • Ideal for couples, families and friends

Customize Your Route

Prefer more coast or fewer cities? We adapt. Link this plan with luxury Morocco tours, honeymoon tours or family tours.

01

Arrive Marrakech

VIP airport transfer, check-in at luxury riad, evening orientation walk in the medina.

02

Marrakech Highlights

Private tour: Bahia Palace, souks, Majorelle Garden and Jemaa el-Fnaa at dusk.

03

Atlas & Valleys

Scenic drive through the High Atlas, Berber village lunch, overnight in Dades or Skoura.

04

Road to Merzouga

Kasbahs, palm groves and Todra Gorge — arrive for sunset camel trek on Erg Chebbi.

05

Sahara Luxury Camp

Sunrise on the dunes, premium desert camp, stargazing and gourmet dinner.

06

Merzouga → Fes

Cross the Ziz Valley and Middle Atlas cedar forests to imperial Fes.

07

Fes Medina

Private guided walk: tanneries, madrasas, artisans and hidden fondouk courtyards.

08

Fes → Chefchaouen

Morning departure to the Blue Pearl — photo stops and riad check-in.

09

Chefchaouen & Coast

Explore blue lanes, then descend toward Casablanca or Rabat.

10

Casablanca & Departure

Hassan II Mosque visit, Atlantic lunch and transfer to CMN airport.
